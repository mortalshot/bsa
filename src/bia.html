LECTURE SHELDULE

The Baltic International Academy (BIA) stands as a prominent non-governmental institution of higher education within the Baltic States, originating in 1992. Recognized as an accredited Higher Educational Institution (HEI) in Latvia, our programs hold global recognition. Offering a spectrum of 26 study programs across 8 distinct directions, we cater to diverse educational pursuits.

Dear student! The class schedule is available on your personal account “My BIA” where you also have the opportunity to receive information  news, academic achievements and financial data.
To sign in a student portal (in test mode) “My BIA”, you need the following information: LOGIN – email; PASSWORD – bsa-number of student agreement with BIA in capital letters (eg: bsa-RI0A0000).
