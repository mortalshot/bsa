The Baltic International Academy (BIA) stands as a prominent non-governmental institution of higher education within the Baltic States, originating in 1992. Recognized as an accredited Higher Educational Institution (HEI) in Latvia, our programs hold global recognition. Offering a spectrum of 26 study programs across 8 distinct directions, we cater to diverse educational pursuits.
