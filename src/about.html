The Baltic International Academy (BIA) stands as a prominent non-governmental institution of higher education within the Baltic States, originating in 1992. Recognized as an accredited Higher Educational Institution (HEI) in Latvia, our programs hold global recognition. Offering a spectrum of 26 study programs across 8 distinct directions, we cater to diverse educational pursuits.

At BIA, education spans various levels, ranging from College Programs to Doctoral studies, nurturing a total student body of 2057 individuals, including 1046 full-time students, with a proud alumni base exceeding 30,000 graduates. Embracing diversity, our multinational environment welcomes 500 foreign students from over 30 countries, fostering a rich cultural exchange.

Our international outreach extends through nearly 400 cooperation agreements with universities worldwide, notably 145 under the esteemed ERASMUS+ program, encompassing 25 European countries. Distinguished scholars and industry professionals, totaling 110 professors holding PhD degrees, conduct lectures, seminars, and masterclasses, enriching the academic experience.

Beyond academia, BIA boasts five Cultural and Information Centers, including the German Center, the Nordic Center, and more, along with dedicated spaces such as the Art Gallery "BiArt," fostering artistic expression and cultural appreciation.

Our commitment to holistic student welfare is evidenced through robust physical infrastructure, featuring scientific libraries, numerous reading halls, cafeterias, computer labs, and a state-of-the-art conference center named "Lomonosov." Moreover, strong ties with Latvian and international organizations underscore our engagement with the broader community and business sector.

In essence, BIA University stands as a gateway to a world of educational excellence, cultural enrichment, and professional growth, inviting individuals to embark on a journey of knowledge and discovery.
